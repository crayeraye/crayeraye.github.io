I have started to read again!

I have always been affraid of words; reading and listening to words I do not understand, whilst frightened to even ask what they may mean, because this to me meant hard work, a display of my non-existant intelligance, no I choose to not adventure. This is how I have spent the last decade of my life, fearing words.

Now do I truly understand the strength and the power words have had on me my entire life.

There have been many times in my life where self-development has been my driving quest.

Self-development is what always brings me back to reading, and this time it has been a beauitful return.

If you like to read with me

This is an ode to many words that I have connected and have helped me on my recent journey.

* The Confidence Gap - Dr Russ Harris

* Habits Aren't Just For Nuns - Robyn Pulman

* Black Elk Speaks - John G. Neihardt

* The Four Agreemenets - Don Miguel Ruiz
